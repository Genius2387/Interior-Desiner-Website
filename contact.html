Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us

How do I get started with my project?

Simply fill out our contact form or give us a call to schedule your initial consultation. We'll discuss your vision, requirements, and budget to determine the best approach for your project.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer a complimentary 30-minute initial consultation where we discuss your project goals and how we can help. Detailed design consultations are part of our paid services.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Pune and surrounding areas within a 50km radius. For exceptional projects, we're happy to discuss travel arrangements for locations further away.

How quickly can you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly at +91 98765 43210.

Can I visit your studio?

Absolutely! We welcome studio visits by appointment. This allows us to show you material samples, discuss your project in detail, and give you a feel for our design aesthetic.
