Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and design process

How long does a typical interior design project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A single room design typically takes 4-6 weeks, while full home projects can take 3-6 months from concept to completion.

Do you work with existing furniture and decor?

Absolutely! We love incorporating pieces that have sentimental value or are in good condition. We'll assess what works with the new design and suggest updates where needed.

What's included in your design fee?

Our design fee covers initial consultation, space planning, design development, material selection, 3D visualizations, and detailed specifications. Implementation costs are separate.

Can you work within my budget?

Yes! We work with various budgets and will prioritize elements that give you the biggest impact. We're transparent about costs and help you make informed decisions.

Do you offer virtual design services?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations and e-design services for clients who prefer remote collaboration or are located outside our service area.
